Physics and Astronomy Club

Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee

The Physics and Astronomy Club (PaAC), IIT Roorkee, is a vibrant hub for scientific discourse and academic exploration, uniting students who share a passion for physics, mathematics, and astronomy. Whether you're fascinated by the mysteries of the universe or intrigued by the logic of mathematical theorems, PaAC provides a platform to learn, discuss, and engage with like-minded individuals. From **stargazing sessions and astrophotography workshops to guest lectures, student-led discussions, and competitive events like the Integration Bee, PaAC fosters a culture of curiosity and knowledge-sharing. As a student-driven club, we actively welcome new ideas—whether it’s a lecture, workshop, or any academic initiative, we encourage members to collaborate and bring their visions to life. If you are passionate about science, academia, and exploration, join us in our journey through space and time!
